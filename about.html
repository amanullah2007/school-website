Our Story

For over three decades, Excelsior Academy has been dedicated to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow.

At Excelsior Academy, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and holistic approach ensure that every student discovers their true potential.

Our History :

Founded in 1985 by Dr. Elizabeth Mitchell, Excelsior Academy began with just 50 students and 5 dedicated teachers. Our founders envisioned a school that would challenge traditional educational paradigms and create a nurturing environment where students could truly thrive.


Over the years, we've grown into a leading educational institution with state-of-the-art facilities, exceptional faculty, and a community of over 800 students. Throughout our evolution, we've remained true to our founding principles while continuously adapting to prepare students for an ever-changing world.

Our Mission :

At Excelsior Academy, our mission is to inspire and empower students to become compassionate, responsible, and innovative thinkers who contribute positively to society. We believe in cultivating a growth mindset and fostering intellectual curiosity that extends beyond the classroom.


We strive to provide a balanced education that honors academic rigor while nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and character development. Our approach recognizes that true education encompasses not just what students know, but who they become.
